Why Are Aquatic Landscapes Important?

Aquatic landscapes are essential for life, supporting the planet's delicate balance. Oceans produce over half of the Earth's oxygen, while coral reefs act as biodiversity hotspots, housing millions of marine species. Wetlands and mangroves play vital roles in protecting coastal regions from floods and erosion.
